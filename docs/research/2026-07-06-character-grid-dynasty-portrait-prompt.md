## Prompt

Generate one high-resolution 3x3 portrait grid image.

Task type: 3x3 dynasty and era portrait series.

Goal:
Create a single 3x3 grid showing the same fictional Chinese woman across nine historical and modern eras. The image should work as a cultural creative calendar page or long-form storyboard reference. The nine panels must read as one coherent portrait series, not nine unrelated people.

Subject:
A fictional Chinese woman around 30 years old. She has deep brown to black hair, adapted per era as coiled hair, tied hair, short waves, or loose modern hair. She has an oval face, warm light-to-medium skin tone, deep brown eyes, soft natural brows, balanced understated facial features, and a typical East Asian facial structure with a relatively straight nose bridge and not overly sculpted facial volume. Her overall temperament is gentle, reserved, calm, and inwardly elegant.

Identity consistency:
Across all nine panels, preserve the same face proportions, bone structure, skin tone, eye shape, brow shape, mouth shape, and restrained temperament. Only hairstyle, clothing, accessories, pose angle, and setting change by era. Avoid making the panels look like different races, different ages, or different people.

Visual style:
High-resolution semi-realistic oil paint blended with polished digital retouching. Soft studio lighting, consistent white balance across the full grid. Each panel may have a subtle paper or silk texture appropriate to the era, but the whole image must share one unified painterly finish.

Layout:
3x3 grid, nine half-body portraits. Use very thin 1px light-gray dividers and a subtle 8px rounded-corner feeling on the outer grid. Divider lines must remain narrow and elegant, never thicker than 2% of the image width. Each panel should have natural eye direction, either looking slightly off-camera or subtly angled. Avoid copy-pasted identical head angles; introduce small pose and gaze variations while keeping the same person.

Panels:
1. Row 1, column 1: Han dynasty. Cross-collar shenyi robe, simplified buyao hair ornament, seated atmosphere, background with bamboo slips and a low table.
2. Row 1, column 2: Tang dynasty. High bun, qixiong ruqun, flowing pibo scarf, lotus-pattern screen behind her.
3. Row 1, column 3: Song dynasty. Duijin long gown, subtle pearl makeup, refined study room background with lattice window and plum blossom vase.
4. Row 2, column 1: Yuan dynasty. Simplified gugu crown, long robe, softly blurred grassland yurt curtain scenery.
5. Row 2, column 2: Ming dynasty. Standing-collar long ao jacket, mamian skirt visible in half-body framing, garden moon window or decorative lattice window.
6. Row 2, column 3: Qing dynasty. Everyday qizhuang robe, one earring with three pearls, softly blurred inner-court corridor pillars.
7. Row 3, column 1: Republican era. Short wavy hair, standing-collar short jacket, holding a rattan suitcase, period calendar-poster style background.

Historical and cultural constraints:
Keep clothing and accessories broadly plausible for each era; do not mix eras in a way that creates obvious historical errors. Do not use text labels inside the panels. Do not add watermarks, signatures, captions, UI elements, or app screenshots.

Safety and likeness constraints:
The subject must be fictional. Do not intentionally resemble any specific real public figure or celebrity. Do not copy Taylor Swift or any named public person's facial details; only use the general idea of a single identity shown across a time-axis grid.

Quality constraints:
Faces should be sharp and expressive. Hands, jewelry, and fabric details should be clean and not malformed. Keep the grid balanced, with each panel equally sized and aligned. The final image should be a square composition suitable for a profile-series presentation.
